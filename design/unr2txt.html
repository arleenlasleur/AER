Conventional text adapter for unrealed, v. 0.1-2024.05.01 by Arleen Lasleur.


  How to prepare text:
  1. Edit in FAR.
  2. Press F11 -> Align block.
  3. Use settings:
     [ 34] Right margin
     [x]   Reformat block    
     [x]   Do not leave short
     [ ]   Justify right border
  4. Hyphenize at your will.
